ReadyCLOUD
The ReadyCLOUD feature gives you remote access over the Internet to a USB storage device that is connected to your router's USB port.
How to Set Up ReadyCLOUD
Step 1:You need a ReadyCLOUD account. If you do not have one, click here to create an account and select "Create Account".
Step 2:Go to router GUI http://routerlogin.net ReadySHARE page, select ReadyCLOUD option, and enter your ReadyCLOUD user name and password to register your router with ReadyCLOUD.
Step 3:Log back into http://readycloud.netgear.com with your ReadyCLOUD account. The USB device that is connected to your router displays.
Note: You can use a supplementary remote application to make a secure connection from your computer or mobile device to the router's USB device.
Log in to this remote application and you can access the USB device from anywhere. click here to get the remote application.

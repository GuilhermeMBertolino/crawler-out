Quick Setup - Failed To Detect The Internet Connection Type
The router is failed to detect your internet connection type, the following are the most likely reasons:
1. The hardware connection is wrong or the modem is not ready.
2. Your Internet connection type is Dynamic IP but the router needs to clone the MAC address of the original computer.
3. Your ISP(Internet Service Provider) assigned a fixed(static) IP to you.
